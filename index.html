Our Story

A Farm Born from a
Peruvian Culture

Peruvian heritage

Nestled in the rolling hills of Stem, North Carolina, Camacho's Farms is a labor of love from a family with deep roots in Peru. Since 2020, we have shared our passion for animals, community, and Latin culture with everyone who walks through our gates.

Our farm is home to a herd of gentle alpacas and playful goats — gentle souls who have a way of making every guest smile. We opened our doors not just as a farm, but as a venue where memories are made: weddings, birthdays, baby showers, and celebrations honoring the rich tapestry of Latin American culture.
