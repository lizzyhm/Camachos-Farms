# Camachos-Farms

Camachos Farms is a family owned farm and outdoor venue. There are goats and alpacas on the farm as well as a venue space for family friendly events.

The family loves to immerse themselves and spread their Peruvian culture through events for the Peruvian independence day or cultural events like their Peruvian Fusion event, where they partnered with multiple companies and organization to not just bring the Peruvian culture to the community, but to also allow unity among the community through a game of soccer or family friendly entertainment.

This website is to not only show the culture and unity that Camacho's Farms can bring, but to also allow access to participate among the community they provide by staying up to date on their events or events provided by companies and organizations that they are partnering with now or have partnered with in the past.
